Material Testing

  • Cube Test Measures the compressive strength of concrete by testing cube-shaped samples, ensuring the material meets design specifications.
  • Brick Testing Evaluates the quality, strength, and water absorption capacity of bricks to ensure their suitability for construction.
  • Cement Testing Analyzes properties like setting time, fineness, and strength of cement to ensure compliance with industry standards.
  • Concrete Testing Determines the workability, strength, and durability of concrete mixtures used in construction projects.
  • Steel Testing Assesses the tensile strength, ductility, and elasticity of steel to verify its performance under various loads.
  • Bitumen Testing Examines the quality and properties of bitumen, such as viscosity and penetration, to ensure reliable road construction.

Structural Testing

  • Load Testing Checks the structural integrity by applying loads to assess its ability to bear weight under designed conditions.
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Inspects materials and structures for defects without causing damage, ensuring safety and reliability.
  • Deflection Testing Measures the displacement or bending of a structure under load to evaluate its flexibility and strength.
  • Flexural Testing Tests the bending strength of materials like beams to ensure they can withstand expected stresses.

Geotechnical Testing

  • Standard Penetration Test (SPT) Determines soil density and strength by measuring resistance to a standardized penetration.
  • Cone Penetration Test (CPT) Evaluates soil properties by driving a cone into the ground and measuring resistance and friction.
  • Pile Load Test Assesses the load-bearing capacity of foundation piles under static conditions.
  • Pile Integrity Test (PIT) Detects structural soundness and potential flaws in foundation piles using wave propagation techniques.
  • Pile Dynamic Analysis (PDA) Measures the dynamic response of piles to evaluate load capacity and soil resistance.
  • Friction Testing Determines the frictional resistance of bored piles for accurate foundation design.
  • Radial Load Test Tests lateral load-bearing capacity, often used in soil stabilization and foundation analysis.
